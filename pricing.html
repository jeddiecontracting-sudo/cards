Questions
What's included in the free plan? ▼
Free users get limited card scanning, eBay listing generation, and live TCGPlayer market prices. AI condition grading (PSA estimate) and selling on the Card Radar marketplace require Premium.
How does the 2% commission work? ▼
When you sell a card through the Card Radar marketplace, we take 2% of the final sale price plus your payment processor fee. That's it — no listing fees, no final value fees beyond that.
Can I cancel my subscription? ▼
Yes, cancel anytime from your account settings. You'll keep premium access until the end of your billing period.
What cards does it support? ▼
Currently optimized for Pokémon TCG with live pricing from TCGPlayer. New card sets update automatically — no manual updates needed.
